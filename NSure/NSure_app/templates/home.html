NSure's Insurance Plan Advisor

NSure provides you advice for selecting your insurance plans at an instant through Machine Learning technology without requiring you to reveal any sensitive personal information.

How it works:
  • Tracks the characteristics of 18 Insurance plans offered among six of the best health insurers operating in the Dutch marlet
  • Identifies how other clients select their insurance plans to build an understanding behind customer preference and product choices
  • By gauging your preferences, NSure is capable of personally advising you on the top 3 insurance plans that we think suit you best along with a matching score used to describe how close of a match there is between your preferences and the chocie of insurance plan
  • Avoids you unnecessary costs from insurance brokers whilst delivering quality advice at an instant.

Preference Field Descriptions

To ensure we give you the best advice possible, we kindly ask you to share your preferences regarding what you're seeking in an insurance plan. This will enable us to provide tailored recommendations based on your specific needs and preferences. Your input will greatly help us guide you towards the most suitable insurance policy.

Base cost of Insurance in € per month:
Refers to the price of the insurance that you are willing to pay without any further additional insurance benefits or policies being bundled along the health insurance policy.

Payment Frequency:
Payment Frequency refers to how you prefer to manage the payment structure of your insurance policy. It determines how often you make payments for the policy. For instance, if you choose a monthly payment frequency, it means you prefer to pay a fee every month throughout the duration of your active policy contract.

Non-Contracted healthcare reimbursement rate:
Non-contracted healthcare refers to choosing a healthcare provider who isn't partnered with your insurance company. In such cases, you'll receive partial reimbursement for the expenses. The portion of costs exceeding the reimbursement rate will require you to personally cover those expenses from your own pocket.

Choice of add-on insurance benefits:
Insurance policies offer various additional health insurance benefits that you might find valuable and worth choosing. These extra benefits usually come with an additional cost on top of the base insurance policy premium.

Dental insurance benefits:
This preference field directly relates to the choice of insurance benefits add-ons related to dental care.

Physiotherapy insurance benefits:
This preference field directly related to the choice of insurance benefits add-ons related to physiotherapy care.

Mental-health insurance benefits:
This field indicates the policy's coverage of mental healthcare related costs and the selection of add-ons which are related to mental-health.

Coverage for urgent care abroad:
States whether you recieve coverage for urgent care whilst being abroad from the Netherlands.
